At Leuleo Instant Foods, we believe everyone deserves access to real, wholesome Ethiopian cuisine — no matter how busy life gets. That's why we created Zede Ethiopian Instant Foods.

Born in the vibrant city of Addis Ababa, Zede captures the soul of Ethiopian cooking in convenient, instant formats. Our tagline "HEPAATREY & MYRITOM GOTHT" reflects our core promise: Tasty, Instant and Hassle Free.
