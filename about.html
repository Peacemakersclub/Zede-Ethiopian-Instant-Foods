About Us

From Ethiopian kitchens to the world — our story of flavor and convenience.

Our Story

At Leuleo Instant Foods, we believe everyone deserves access to real, wholesome Ethiopian cuisine — no matter how busy life gets. That's why we created Zede Ethiopian Instant Foods.

Born in the vibrant city of Addis Ababa, Zede captures the soul of Ethiopian cooking in convenient, instant formats. Our recipes draw from generations of tradition, using authentic spice blends like berbere and slow-cooked stew techniques — now ready in just five minutes.

Our tagline "HEPAATREY & MYRITOM GOTHT" reflects our core promise: Tasty, Instant and Hassle Free.
